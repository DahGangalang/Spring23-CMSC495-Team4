***Section: Header
Title: Grilled Cheese with pickles
Author: Amelia Black
Tag: sandwich
Tag: bread, cheese, pickles
***Section: Ingredients
2 slices whole grain bread or bread of choice
1 slice of cheese
2 slices of dill pickles
1 tablespoon of butter
***Section: Instructions
Step 1 Preheat saucepan on medium heat. Add the butter and begin melting it. 
Step 2 Add the cheese to one of the slices of bread, and lay both slices in the saucepan. 
Step 3 After 3-5 minutes add the pickles on top of the cheese and add the lone slice to the rest of the sandwich. 
Step 4 Cut the sandwich in half and enjoy!
