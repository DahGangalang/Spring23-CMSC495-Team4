***Section: Header
Title: Avocado Toast
Author: Amelia Black
Tag: toast
Tag: avocado
***Section: Ingredients
1 avocado peeled and seeded
2 tablespoons chopped cilantro
juice from 1 lime
salt & pepper to taste
2 slices whole grain bread or bread of choice
***Section: Instructions
Step 1 Toast 2 slices of whole grain in a toaster until golden and crispy.
Step 2 In a small bowl combine and mash the avocado, cilantro, lime, and salt + pepper to taste. Spread half of the mixture on each slice of toasted bread.
