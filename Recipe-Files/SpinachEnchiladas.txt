***Section: Header
Title: Spinach Enchiladas 
Author: Amelia Black
Tag: enchiladas
Tag: spinach, vegetarian
***Section: Ingredients
2 cloves of minced garlic 
1 package (10 ounce) of frozen chopped spinach thawed drain and squeezed dry
2 cups of shredded Monterey Jack cheese
10 (6 inch) corn tortillas
1 (19 ounce can enchilada sauce
***Section: Instructions
Step 1 Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F. 
Step 2 Add garlic to saucepan over medium heat for 2 to 3 minutes. Stir in spinach and cook for 5 minutes. Remove from the heat and mix in 1 cup of Monterey Jack cheese. 
Step 3 Warm one tortilla in a skillet over medium heat, for about 15 seconds/ Re[eat to warm remaining tortillas. Spoon about 1/4 cup of spinach mixture into the center of each tortilla, roll tortilla around filling, and place into a 9x13 inch baking dish. Pour enchilada sauce over their top and sprinkle with the remaining 1 cup of Monterey Jack.  
Step 4 Bake in preheated oven until the sauce is bubbling and cheese is lightly browned at the edges, 15 to 20 minutes.
