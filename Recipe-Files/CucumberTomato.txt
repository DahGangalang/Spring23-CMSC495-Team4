***Section: Header
Title: Cucumber Tomato Salad
Author: Amelia Black
Tag: salad
Tag: cucumber, tomato 
***Section: Ingredients
3 English cucumbers, sliced into quarters
2 vine ripe tomatoes, sliced into quarters
1 red onion, sliced
2 tablespoons of red wine vinegar
3 tablespoons of extra-virgin olive oil
Salt and pepper
***Section: Instructions
Step 1 Combine all ingredients in a bowl. 
Step 2 Dress with vinegar and oil, salt and pepper, to your taste.
