***Section: Header
Title: Chicken Noodle Soup
Author: Amelia Black
Tag: soup
Tag: chicken, noodle
Tags: celery, carrots
***Section: Ingredients
4 cans of chicken broth
1 pound of chopped cooked chicken breast
1 cup of sliced celery
1 cup of egg noodles
1 cup of sliced carrots
***Section: Instructions
Step 1 Add onion and celery to pot and cook on medium heat until tender. 
Step 2 Add chicken broth, egg noodles, and carrots. 
Step 3 Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
